Fountain of Peace

Why did you choose this place to capture?

The garden's Fountain of SHS in San Nicolas III, positioned within the garden, stands out as one of the finest locations in my view. The fountain has significantly influenced the overall aesthetics of the garden. However, a notable drawback is the poor cleanliness of both the environment and the fountain water. The neglect of regular cleaning has resulted in discolored, green water, creating an unpleasant visual impression. Nonetheless, if efforts are directed towards maintaining cleanliness and improving the water flow system, I am confident that this garden could become one of the premier spots within SHS in San Nicolas III.
